Becoming a collaborator

being nominated to our brain trust

The n0 project is looking for collaborators who are interested in distributed systems research. n0 is a platform that curates research groups who are mutually invested in research goals. The single hardest problem when doing research is separating signal from noise. n0 believes that smaller groups with stronger connections have a better signal-to-noise ratio. As a n0 collaborator, you will have direct access to n0 staff. To become a n0 collaborator, you must be nominated by another collaborator and seconded. n0 is also looking for contributors. The best way to get an invite is to become a collaborator on their GitHub.
